# 🎙️ SRM Events 2K26 - Speaker Pitch Script

*(This document is written conversationally. You can read it directly or use it as a guide for your presentation.)*

---

### 1. Introduction (The Hook)
"Hi everyone, my name is Harsh Yadav. Today, I'm excited to present **SRM Events 2K26**. 

In short, it's a modern, centralized platform designed to handle all campus events, hackathons, and club activities right here at SRM University."

### 2. The Problem
"Before we talk about the platform, let’s talk about the problem. 

Right now, as a student, finding out what’s going on around campus is chaotic. We have to scroll through endless WhatsApp groups, check random Instagram pages, or look at crowded physical notice boards. Information is scattered. Students end up missing registration deadlines, and clubs struggle to market their events effectively and track who’s attending."

### 3. The Solution
"That’s exactly why I built SRM Events 2K26. 

We’ve created a single, beautifully designed application that brings the entire campus pulse into one place. 

If you are a student, you simply log in, browse through categorized events—whether they are technical, cultural, or sports—and register with just a single click. 

If you are looking for a community, we have a unified Club Directory featuring over 22 active student clubs where you can see exactly what they do and what events they have coming up."

### 4. The Technology (Keep it brief & impressive)
"To make this experience as smooth and fast as possible, we focused heavily on using a modern tech stack. 

The frontend is a blazing-fast Single Page Application built with **React 19** and **TypeScript**, styled beautifully with **TailwindCSS** and **shadcn/ui** to give it a premium dark-mode feel. 

On the backend, we built a secure **Node.js and Express** REST API. We also implemented stateless **JWT Authentication** and password hashing so that every student's data and registrations are kept completely secure."

### 5. Future Roadmap
"While the platform is already fully functional, we have big plans for version 2.0. We plan to integrate a proper database like MongoDB, add Razorpay for paid event ticketing, and even implement real-time notifications and QR code check-ins for the actual day of the event."

### 6. Conclusion
"Ultimately, SRM Events 2K26 isn't just a website. It is a digital ecosystem designed to connect students with opportunities, streamline club management, and elevate the campus culture. 

Thank you, and I’d be happy to show you a demo or answer any questions!"
